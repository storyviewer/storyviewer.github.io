How to Cancel Auto-Renewable Subscription in iOS

Follow these steps to cancel your auto-renewable subscription:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iOS device.
  2. Tap on your Apple ID at the top of the screen.
  3. Tap Subscriptions.
  4. Find and tap on the subscription for our app.
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription. If you donâ€™t see this option, the subscription is already canceled and won't renew.

Note: Cancelling a subscription will stop the auto-renewal, but you will still have access to the subscription's features until the end of the current billing period.

Seeing Ads After Purchasing Subscription

If you have paid for a subscription but are still seeing ads, you may need to restore your purchases. This can happen in some cases, such as reinstalling the app or using a new device.

  1. Open our app on your iOS device.
  2. Go to the Subscription page within the app.
  3. Click on the Restore Purchases button.

This should synchronize your purchase with your device, removing any ads if your subscription is still active. If you continue to experience issues, please contact our support team for assistance.
